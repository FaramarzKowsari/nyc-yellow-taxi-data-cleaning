# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and free from harassment.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and evidence.
- Document assumptions and acknowledge uncertainty.
- Respect privacy, licensing, and data-governance requirements.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate misinformation, and unauthorized disclosure of private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate these expectations and may restrict participation when necessary.
